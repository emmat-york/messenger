Telegram desktop

Telegram is a cloud-based instant messaging app known for its speed, security, and versatility. It allows users to send text messages, voice messages, multimedia files, and documents of various formats. Telegram supports both private chats and group conversations, with the ability to host groups of up to 200,000 members and channels for broadcasting messages to unlimited audiences.

Key features include end-to-end encryption for secret chats, cross-platform accessibility, and powerful customization options. Telegram also offers bots, which can automate tasks, provide information, or create interactive experiences. With a focus on privacy and innovation, Telegram has gained popularity as a reliable alternative to traditional messaging apps.
